6th March, 1957. On 6th March, 1957 the Prime Minister of Ghana, Dr Kwame Nkrumah declared the country free from British Colonial rule. Ghana set a record as the first country to gain independence from the colonial rule after the long fight for freedom. Since that year, the country has been celebrating Independence Day on March 6 every year.
